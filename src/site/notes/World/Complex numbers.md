#math 

z= a + ib in complex plane
In polar coordinates - $(r, \theta)$ (radius, angle)
$(a,b) = (r cos\theta, r sin\theta)$

## Euler's formula:
$z = r(cos\theta + i sin\theta) = re^{i\theta}$

## Multiplication
$z_{1} z_{2} = (r_{1},\theta_{1})(r_{2},\theta_{2}) = (r_{1}r_{2},\theta_{1} + \theta_{2})$
